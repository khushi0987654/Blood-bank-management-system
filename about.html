About Us

We at Helping Hands, have created this platform where donors, receivers, and hospital blood banks all come together at one portal. The receiver can directly contact donors and volunteers of Helping Hands in case of any shortage of blood at the blood bank/hospital.


We believe that by automating the existing manual blood bank management system, we can bring all the donors, receivers, hospitals, and blood banks together at one portal to meet their needs efficiently.
